About me

Hi! My name is Owen Frey, I'm a mechanical engineering student at the University of Guelph in Ontario, Canada.

My main passion is motorsports, and I am currently pursuing a career in motorsports engineering. I am the suspension lead at my University's FSAE team and I am helping out teams in the FEL Motorsports Radical Cup Canada, and Canadian Sports Compact Series. In 2023, I was one of 6 interviewees for the suspension design placement with Red Bull Racing. I hope to one day be a design or race engineer in the top tiers of motorsport, be it Formula 1, WEC, or IMSA. In the meantime, I hope that this website can be a useful resource for anyone interested in learning more about vehicle dynamics and suspension design, and if you are interested in the work I do, please reach out to me at owenjfrey@gmail.com
